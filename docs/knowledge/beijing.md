# Beijing Travel Guide

## Overview
Beijing, the capital of China, offers a perfect blend of ancient history and modern culture. Best visited in spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October).

## Must-Visit Attractions

### The Forbidden City
- Hours: 8:30-17:00 (closed Mondays)
- Duration: 3-4 hours
- Ticket: 60 RMB (peak season), 40 RMB (off season)
- Tips: Book online 7 days in advance. Enter through Meridian Gate. Audio guide recommended.

### Great Wall (Badaling Section)
- Hours: 7:30-18:00
- Duration: 4-6 hours (including travel from city center)
- Ticket: 40 RMB
- Tips: Arrive early to avoid crowds. Wear comfortable shoes. Bring water and snacks.

### Temple of Heaven
- Hours: 6:00-21:00
- Duration: 2 hours
- Ticket: 15 RMB
- Tips: Visit in the morning to see locals practicing tai chi and calligraphy.

### Summer Palace
- Hours: 7:00-17:00
- Duration: 3-4 hours
- Ticket: 30 RMB
- Tips: Take a boat ride on Kunming Lake. Best photo spots at Longevity Hill.

## Food Recommendations

### Peking Duck
- Recommended: Quanjude, Da Dong, Bianyifang
- Price: 200-400 RMB per duck
- Tips: Make reservation in advance. Quanjude has the longest history.

### Local Snacks
- Wangfujing Snack Street: Try candied hawthorn, fried dough twists
- Guijie (Ghost Street): Best for late-night dining, spicy crawfish
- Zhajiangmian (Noodles with Soybean Paste): Local staple, found everywhere

## Transportation
- Subway: Extensive network, 3-8 RMB per ride
- Bus: 1-2 RMB, can be slow during rush hour
- Taxi: Starting fare 13 RMB
- Airport Express: 25 RMB from airport to city center

## Best Seasons
- Spring (Mar-May): Mild weather, occasional sandstorms
- Summer (Jun-Aug): Hot and humid, peak tourist season
- Autumn (Sep-Nov): Best season, clear skies and comfortable temperatures
- Winter (Dec-Feb): Cold, but fewer tourists and cheaper prices

## Suggested Itineraries

### 3-Day Classic
Day 1: Forbidden City → Tiananmen Square → Wangfujing
Day 2: Great Wall at Badaling → Summer Palace
Day 3: Temple of Heaven → Nanluoguxiang Hutong → Houhai

### 5-Day In-Depth
Day 1: Tiananmen Square → Forbidden City → Jingshan Park
Day 2: Great Wall at Mutianyu → Bird's Nest
Day 3: Summer Palace → Lama Temple → Ghost Street
Day 4: Temple of Heaven → 798 Art District → Sanlitun
Day 5: Beihai Park → Hutongs → National Museum
