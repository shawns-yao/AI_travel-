# Chengdu Travel Guide

## Overview
Chengdu, capital of Sichuan province, is famous for giant pandas, spicy cuisine, and a laid-back lifestyle. Best visited in spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November).

## Must-Visit Attractions

### Giant Panda Breeding Research Base
- Hours: 7:30-18:00
- Duration: 3-4 hours
- Ticket: 58 RMB
- Tips: Arrive before 8:30 AM to see pandas at their most active (feeding time). Morning is best.

### Jinli Ancient Street
- Hours: 24 hours (shops typically 10:00-22:00)
- Duration: 1-2 hours
- Ticket: Free
- Tips: Best visited in the evening for the lantern-lit atmosphere. Try local snacks here.

### Wuhou Shrine
- Hours: 8:00-18:00
- Duration: 1.5-2 hours
- Ticket: 60 RMB
- Tips: Dedicated to Zhuge Liang and Liu Bei of the Three Kingdoms period. History lovers will enjoy this.

### People's Park
- Hours: 6:00-22:00
- Duration: 1-2 hours
- Ticket: Free
- Tips: Visit Heming Tea House inside the park for an authentic Chengdu tea experience. Matchmaking corner on weekends is interesting.

### Mount Qingcheng
- Hours: 8:00-17:30
- Duration: Full day trip
- Ticket: 90 RMB
- Tips: Birthplace of Taoism. Front mountain is more popular, back mountain offers better hiking.

## Food Recommendations

### Hot Pot
- Recommended: Shujiuxiang, Xiaolongkan, Haidilao
- Price: 80-150 RMB per person
- Tips: Chengdu hot pot uses lots of Sichuan peppercorns (ma la). Order yuan yang guo (half spicy/half mild) if unsure.

### Local Specialties
- Mapo Tofu: Chen Mapo Tofu (oldest and most famous)
- Dan Dan Noodles: Street stalls and small restaurants
- Sichuan Cold Noodles: Perfect for summer
- Chuan Chuan Xiang (skewer hot pot): Budget-friendly, 0.5-2 RMB per skewer

## Transportation
- Metro: 7 lines covering main attractions, 2-8 RMB
- Bus: 1-2 RMB
- Taxi/Didi: Starting fare 8-9 RMB
- Airport: Shuangliu International Airport, Metro Line 10 connects to city

## Best Seasons
- Spring (Mar-May): Comfortable, flowers blooming
- Summer (Jun-Aug): Hot and humid, but great for mountain trips
- Autumn (Sep-Nov): Best season, golden ginkgo leaves
- Winter (Dec-Feb): Cold and damp, fewer tourists

## Suggested Itineraries

### 3-Day Panda & Food
Day 1: Panda Base → People's Park → Jinli Ancient Street
Day 2: Wuhou Shrine → Kuanzhai Alley → Hot Pot dinner
Day 3: Mount Qingcheng day trip

### 5-Day Chengdu & Surroundings
Day 1: Panda Base → Sichuan University area food tour
Day 2: Wuhou Shrine → Jinli → Du Fu's Thatched Cottage
Day 3: Mount Qingcheng day trip
Day 4: Leshan Giant Buddha day trip (2 hours by train)
Day 5: Tea house morning → Local market → Shu embroidery workshop
